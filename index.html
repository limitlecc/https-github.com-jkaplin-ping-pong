ft_ping-pong

    Weekly Meetups
  • Join us in the Multipurpose Room from 4 - 6 PM on Fridays for some fun ping pong games.
  • We'll play games like 'Run Around The Table', 'The Iron Throne' and many other ping pong games.

    • Monthly Tournament
  • On the first Firday of the month at 6 PM we will generate the first bracket for the tournament.
  • Remember to sign up for the tournament before that first bracket.
  • Once the tournament starts, every week you will have until Thursday Midnight to arrange a game with your opponent and update the results.
  • If you did not manage to signup for the tournament on time feel free to contact us and we will insert you somehow into the losers section.
